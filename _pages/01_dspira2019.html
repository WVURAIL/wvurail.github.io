Digital Signal Processing in Radio Astronomy 2018

Digital Signal Processing in Radio Astronomy (DSPIRA) is an NSF Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) in Engineering and Computer Science Site at the West Virginia University Lane Department of Computer Science and Engineering. This page is a brief discription of 2019 program

This program was attended by a group of six excellent highly motivated teachers from all over the country. The participants included:
